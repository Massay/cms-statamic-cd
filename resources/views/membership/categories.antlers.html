Join/Renew

General Members comprise of the executive and official members, who can elect or nominate members to be elected.

Official Member

Official members are members who contribute towards their membership based on the amount set by the community at a particular time; membership contributions provide them access to privileges not available to the general members.

Nominated Member

Informal members are members of the organization who share the same interest and participate in the organization's events and activities
